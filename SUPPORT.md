# WalkWithUs - Support

## About the App

WalkWithUs connects you with your community through walking. Find local walks, meet neighbors, and stay active together.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I create an account?
Tap "Sign in with Google" or "Sign in with Apple" on the login screen to create your account.

### How do I find walks near me?
After signing in, set your city in your profile. The app will show walks available in your area.

### How do I book a walk?
Browse available walks on the Home screen, tap on a walk to see details, and tap "Book" to reserve your spot.

### How do I cancel a booking?
Go to your Bookings tab, find the walk, and tap "Cancel Booking".

### How do I create a walk?
Walk creation is available to approved organizers. Contact us if you're interested in organizing walks.
